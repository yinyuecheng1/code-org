## Andrea Pisani

[apisani@washtenawisd.org](mailto: apisani@washtenawisd.org)

She was always encouraged to explore the natural world around her and eventually followed her passion for learning and discovering new things by becoming a teacher. Andrea taught mathematics at both the secondary and post-secondary levels for over a decade before taking her current position as Supervisor of Instruction for Mathematics and Science at the Washtenaw Intermediate School District in Ann Arbor, MI.
