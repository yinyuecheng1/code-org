Card Sorting by "Hand"

GOAL: get the cards into sorted order with the fewest number of 'looks' and 'swaps' as you can.

DIRECTIONS:

  1. Click a card to show its face
  2. If two cards are face up click 'swap' to swap thier positions
  3. double-clicking anywhere attempts to swap

RULES:

  1. you can only see at most two cards at once
  2. you can only swap if two cards are selected.
  3. your score doesn't count if you show all cards.

Your browser does not support the canvas tag.
